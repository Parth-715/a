Interesting facts about cranes

Cranes are powerful flyers, with some having the ability to fly over the Himalayas. During migration cranes fly with their head and neck straight out and their feet and legs straight behind them. ... Cranes form lifelong monogamous pair bonds. The mating dance of the crane is spectacular.

Interesting facts about frogs

There are 6,000 species of frogs across the world. ... They display vibrant colours. ... Frogs can drink water through their skin. ... They can jump 20 times their body length. ... Frogs hibernate during winters. ... Some of them are poisonous.
